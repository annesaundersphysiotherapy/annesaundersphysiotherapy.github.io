About Anne Saunders Physiotherapy

Anne Saunders Physiotherapist

I qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist from Sheffield Hallam University in 2001. Since then I have worked in both the NHS and private sector specialising in the assessment and treatment of musculoskeletal pain and injuries. Over the years I have developed a wealth of hands on experience in treating all manner of pain conditions.

I am also an accredited Matwork Pilates Instructor gaining my qualification through the APPI (Australian Physiotherapy and Pilates Association). I find pilates to be an effective and enjoyable form of exercise for both rehabilitation and fitness, and it can be suitable for all ages and fitness levels.
