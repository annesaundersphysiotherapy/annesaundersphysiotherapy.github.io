About us

Anne Saunders Physiotherapy offers a professional, friendly and accessible musculoskeletal physiotherapy service with the primary aims being to reduce or eliminate pain and maximise the overall health and wellbeing of a person.

As a chartered physiotherapist with over 20 years experience I know how important it is to you to get better as quickly as possible and back to doing the things you love.
